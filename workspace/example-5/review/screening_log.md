## Limitations

Screening performed by a single AI reviewer (LLM). PRISMA recommends dual independent screening. For formal systematic reviews, a human second reviewer should validate a sample of decisions, particularly exclusions.

Additional limitations:
- Only PubMed results were screened (243/403 articles). S2 and OpenAlex results were excluded due to >60% off-topic rate (relevance gate). This may miss unique articles indexed only in those databases.
- 5 articles had no abstract available and were retained based on title/metadata only.
- Non-English articles with English abstracts were excluded per protocol language criterion, potentially missing relevant French/German/Dutch studies.
